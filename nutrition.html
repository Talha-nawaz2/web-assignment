Pre-Workout Supplements

Purpose

Pre-workout supplements enhance energy and endurance during exercise, typically taken 15-30 minutes before workouts.

Common Ingredients:

1. Beta-Alanine

  • Benefits: Improves performance in high-intensity exercise.
  • Mechanism: Increases muscle carnosine levels.
  • Dosage: 4–6g/day for 2–4 weeks.
  • Side Effects: May cause skin tingling.

2. Caffeine

  • Benefits: Enhances performance in both high-intensity and endurance activities.
  • Mechanism: Increases endorphin release.
  • Dosage: 3–6mg/kg body weight.

3. Creatine

  • Benefits: Increases muscle energy.
  • Mechanism: Supports higher training volume.
  • Dosage: Initial 20g/day, then 3–5g/day.

Safety Considerations

Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any supplement regimen.

Post-Workout Supplements

Purpose

Post-workout supplements aim to enhance muscle repair, recovery, and growth after exercise.

Common Ingredients:

1. Carbohydrates

  • Importance: Replenishes glycogen stores.
  • Recommendations: 6–12 g carbohydrates/kg body weight after exercise.

2. Protein

  • General Intake: 0.8g/kg body weight.
  • Protein Sources: Includes casein, whey, and plant-based options.

3. Branched-Chain Amino Acids (BCAAs)

  • Components: Leucine, isoleucine, and valine.
  • Effectiveness: Mixed results on muscle growth.

4. Chocolate Milk

  • Benefits: Combines protein, carbohydrates, and electrolytes.

5. Electrolytes

  • Importance: Essential for hydration.

Safety Considerations

Always consult a healthcare professional before starting any supplement regimen.
